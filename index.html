Bio
Bio:

I am a storyteller and always have been. Before I’d learned video-editing, I was orchestrating elaborate playground productions. After I’d learned video-editing (and a few other things too), I studied journalism and film at Leiden University.

My first real job was at Volkskrant magazine, making videos about startups and technology. I was (and still am) fascinated by the driven and passionate entrepreneurs building products to enrich our lives and better our world. Ultimately, I realized I was more interested in crafting beautiful stories than in getting the scoop. I wanted to make videos for startups rather than about them. And so I started CineWard – a video production company that makes videos for all sorts of organizations and private individuals.

I believe passion is contagious- it’s my mission to capture the spark that drives my clients, that thing that excites them most about what they’re doing. And to translate that into video.

Documentary work:

In addition to producing video for CineWard, I recently finished a feature documentary called “Yurt till it hurts” Check out the trailer.

What drives me:

I’m tantalized by sculpting story arcs and by capturing breathtaking imagery. I aim to create videos that excite and inspire and tickle the imagination. Videos that move people and that give wings to projects and ideas aiming to better our world. My mission is to live and work mindfully and artfully and to continually grow as a creator (and as a human).
